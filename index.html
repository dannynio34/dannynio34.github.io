About Me

Hello! I am a passionate architect with expertise in modern architecture, architectural rendering, and interior design. Currently, I am an architectural student at Ambo University, expected to graduate this year.

Over the years, I have successfully completed various projects, including residential buildings, mixed-use developments, lodges, high-rise structures, and complex projects such as airports, corridor developments, and campus designs. I am committed to delivering high-quality work that aligns with my clients' goals.

I am proficient in advanced architectural software such as Revit, SketchUp, Blender, Lumion, Enscape, Twinmotion, and V-Ray. This expertise enables me to create visually engaging presentations that bring ideas to life.
